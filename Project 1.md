**Problem Statement:**
To make a ring such that, when worn it lights up.

Components required:

* 2 BC547 (NPN Transistor)
* 270 Ohms resistor
* 100K Ohms resistor
* 2 1.5V Button Cell batteries
* 3mm LED strip / 3 small LEDs from a LED strip in a circular fashion giving a ring like design
